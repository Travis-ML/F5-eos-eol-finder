F5 EoS / EoL Finder

Upload an Excel sales document or BOM. Get the same file back with F5 hardware End-of-Sale / End-of-Software-Development / End-of-Technical-Support dates appended for each F5 line item.

What this does:

  • Walks every cell, finds anything starting with F5-.
  • Looks up the SKU's hardware family in a curated lifecycle database.
  • Appends columns to each row: family, status, EoS, EoSD, EoTS, EoRMA.
  • Service / VE / BIG-IQ / billing lines are tagged but have no hardware EoL of their own.
  • Adds a summary sheet at the front with counts and unknown-SKU review list.
